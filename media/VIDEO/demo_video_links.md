Demonstration Videos



This document provides external video resources associated with the augmented reality teleoperation architecture implemented with Meta Quest 3, Unity, UDP communication, ROS 2, and Xiaomi CyberDog.



The videos serve as complementary visual evidence of the implemented system, including the AR interface, the operator-side visualization environment, and the Unity–Quest interaction workflow.
